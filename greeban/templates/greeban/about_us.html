  • Our Story 2013 - present
    Started with a mission to provide consumers with the freshest possible produce of microgreens and exotic veggies, is one of India's First Vertical Farms.To fulfill this commitment, we use advanced hydroponic techniques to maintain the best quality of our produce In operations since 2017 we have successfully grown 26+ and 33+ varieties Exotic Veggies & Microgreens respectively.

    It all started when we saw people commute from rural houses to urban skyscrapers but the food for this increasing population still came from the decreasing agricultural land. That's when we decided to shift the horizontal rural farm into a vertical urban setup. After multiple research and development we are here with urban farms that can produce one of the best quality and the best quantity of produce per square foot of land, while using 90% less water than traditional farming Having complete control on the climate we open the boundaries to these exotic vegetables.Also, by growing in the city we reduce the transport cost and time, thus providing the freshest possible produce. Since we can grow the same quality produce for the whole year, we don’t have any seasonal spikes in the pricing thus increasing our reliability for the customers.
